In this presentation on the Research Synthesis & Big Data Conference I introduced preregRS: An R Markdown template to preregister research syntheses. The template is available in RStudio after installing the R package preregRS. It is intended to guide the preregistration process and enable compatibility (e.g. to include analysis code, results, embed PDFs, plots).
