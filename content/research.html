Current Projects

  • (Re)Buildung Trust?
    In this project we investigate the effects of open science badges on perceived trustworthiness in journal articles.
  • Capturing beliefs on the relation of theory and practice
    In this project we established and tested an instrument to measure beliefs concerning the relation of theory and practice in the teaching profession.
  • Systematic Review on adaptive teaching
  • Adaptive teaching with tablets (TabletBW)
  • Computer-based formative Feedback on writing
    A meta-analysis on effects and border conditions
  • TPACK self-report assessments
    Another jingle-jangle fallacy? Examining the validity of Technological Pedagogical and Content Knowledge (TPACK) self-report assessments
  • MapOSR: Mapping Open Science Research
    A Systematic Review of Empirical Studies on Open Science
